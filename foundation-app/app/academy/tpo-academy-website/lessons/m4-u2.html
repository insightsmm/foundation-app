The TPO Weekly Rotation

12 minutes Consistency System

The rotation ensures your content is always in the right sequence — Teach building trust, Proof converting it to belief, Offer converting belief to action.

The specific days matter less than the sequence: never post Offer before Teach and Proof in the same week.

DayModeWhy This Slot
MondayTeachAudiences are fresh and receptive to learning. Opens the week with authority.
WednesdayProofMid-week engagement peaks. Proof shows Monday’s lesson works in the real world.
FridayOfferThe audience has been taught and shown proof. They are warm enough to receive an offer.
Content Format Example — A Full TPO Week for a Contractor
Monday (Teach): "3 mistakes Tampa contractors make on Instagram." Wednesday (Proof): "My client’s roofing company booked 14 qualified leads in one month." Friday (Offer): "Need a steady flow of leads from Instagram? Comment FLYWHEEL for the system overview."
Each post does one job, and the week as a whole moves a stranger from awareness to action. This is the rotation you will run every week for the rest of your business.
