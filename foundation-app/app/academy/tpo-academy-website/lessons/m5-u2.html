The Five ManyChat Triggers

12 minutes Conversion + ManyChat

ManyChat turns comment engagement into automated DM conversations — 24 hours a day, without you watching your phone.

When someone comments your keyword, they instantly receive the promised resource and one qualifying question. You configure all five triggers in this lesson.

TPOThe framework breakdownWarm — has seen your content before

FLYWHEELLocal Flywheel overviewHighest intent — considering a done-for-you solution
VISIBLEVisibility strategy guideWarm — knows they have a reach problem
Lab

Build all five keyword automations in ManyChat. Each one delivers its resource link plus one qualifying question that opens a real conversation. Test each by commenting the keyword on a test post before going live.
