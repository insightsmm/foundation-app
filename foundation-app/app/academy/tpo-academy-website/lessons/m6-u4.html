Your 30-Day Operating Plan

12 minutes Optimize + Local Flywheel

The program ends; the system does not. Your 30-day operating plan runs the content engine without weekly coaching.

Weeks 1-2: execute the rotation exactly and collect clean data. Weeks 3-4: repurpose your top performers and adjust CTAs based on which keywords produced the most DMs. The system becomes self-improving.

This Is Where It Starts

Content compounds like interest. The first 30 days build the foundation. Days 31 to 90 start the acceleration. Most people quit in week three. You now have a system designed to carry you past that moment and keep running for years.
