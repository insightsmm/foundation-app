The Content Repurposing Map

10 minutes Optimize + Local Flywheel

Repurposing is amplification, not recycling. One high-performing piece reaches different consumption preferences and new audience segments when reformatted.

One idea can become five assets. This is the math that makes a content calendar sustainable forever.

FromToWhy
Top Reel5-slide CarouselReels reach new audiences; carousels get saved by warm ones
CarouselLong-form caption postSome of your audience reads captions but skips video
Best lineStory + HighlightA single strong line can outperform the original
Same topicNew Reel, fresh anglePeople who missed it see it; people who saw it share it
Exercise
Repurpose Your Best Post
Turn one winner into a week of content.
Identify your highest-saved post from the analytics audit.
Rewrite its core points as a 5-slide carousel outline.
Pull the single best line for a Story frame and a Highlight.
Plan a new Reel on the same topic from the opposite angle.
